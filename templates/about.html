About EcoClassify

Revolutionizing waste management through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with AI-driven tools that make waste sorting and recycling more accurate, efficient, and accessible. We believe that proper waste classification is the first step toward a sustainable future.

Our Vision

A world where every piece of waste is properly classified and recycled, reducing environmental impact and promoting circular economy principles through the power of artificial intelligence and community engagement.

Technology Behind EcoClassify

Deep Learning Model

Our classification system is powered by MobileNetV2, a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network architecture optimized for mobile and web applications. The model has been fine-tuned on a comprehensive dataset of waste images to achieve:

93.67% Accuracy
2,970 Training Images
5 Categories
361K Parameters

Environmental Impact

Reduced Contamination

Proper waste classification reduces contamination in recycling streams, making the recycling process more efficient and cost-effective.

Increased Recycling Rates

Better classification leads to higher recycling rates, reducing the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators.

Education & Awareness

Our platform educates users about proper waste sorting, creating a more environmentally conscious community.

Technical Implementation

Machine Learning

  • Architecture: MobileNetV2 with transfer learning
  • Framework: PyTorch
  • Training: 50 epochs with data augmentation
  • Optimization: Adam optimizer with learning rate scheduling
  • Accuracy: 93.67% on test dataset

Backend & Infrastructure

  • Framework: Flask (Python)
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth
  • Image Processing: PIL/Pillow
  • Deployment: Production-ready with Gunicorn
